How to use accordions

This heading is one of 2 components you’ll need to form an expanding accordion:

  • Accordion heading (this component)
  • End of Accordion

On your app screen, hit enter after the heading and add all the content you would like to be inside the accordion directly underneath the heading. When you’re ready, add an “end of accordion” component. Repeat for any further accordions you may want.

For more information and inspiration, read our Fliplet guide to accordions.
